Tile Export Guide

How to Import Porcelain Tiles from Iran

Complete guide to importing porcelain and ceramic tiles from Iran. Learn about sizes, export services, FOB, CIF and international shipping.

Iran is one of the important ceramic and porcelain tile production hubs in the Middle East. International buyers choose Iranian tiles because of competitive pricing, wide product variety and access to many export-ready manufacturers.

Why Buyers Import Tiles from Iran

Iranian tile factories produce porcelain tiles, ceramic wall tiles, floor tiles, large format tiles and wood look porcelain tiles in many sizes and finishes. For importers and distributors, this creates an opportunity to source competitive products for residential, commercial and project markets.

Popular Export Tile Sizes

Some of the most requested export sizes include 60x60 porcelain tiles, 60x120 large format porcelain tiles, 80x80 porcelain tiles, 100x100 premium porcelain tiles and 20x120 wood look porcelain tiles. These sizes are suitable for distributors, wholesalers, construction companies and retail tile stores.

Export Process

A typical tile import process includes product selection, sample checking, quotation, packaging confirmation, proforma invoice, payment terms, production or stock confirmation, container loading and export documentation.

FOB and CIF Options

Buyers can request FOB quotations when they manage shipping through their own forwarder. CIF quotations are suitable when the buyer wants shipping cost included to the destination port.
