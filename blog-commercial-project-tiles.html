Best Tile Sizes for Commercial Projects

Learn how to choose porcelain and ceramic tile sizes for commercial projects, hotels, offices and construction developments.

Choosing the right tile size for a commercial project affects design, installation, maintenance and long-term performance. Importers and project buyers should select tile formats based on application, traffic level and design goals.

60x60 Porcelain Tiles

60x60 porcelain tiles are one of the most common choices for commercial floors. They offer a modern look, good availability and practical installation.

60x120 Large Format Tiles

60x120 porcelain tiles are ideal for hotels, lobbies, showrooms and premium commercial interiors. Their large size reduces grout lines and creates a high-end appearance.

80x80 and 100x100 Tiles

80x80 and 100x100 porcelain tiles are suitable for spacious areas, luxury projects and modern architectural designs where visual impact is important.

Wall Tile Options

30x60 and 30x90 ceramic wall tiles are widely used for bathrooms, kitchens, hotel walls and decorative commercial spaces.

How to Choose the Right Size

Buyers should consider project type, target market, budget, finish, slip resistance, installation cost and available stock. LuxeCeram helps importers and project buyers select suitable tile sizes for different commercial applications.
